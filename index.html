THE ANSWER ARC

A complete answer travels
from concept to consequence.

Every extended question rewards the same movement. State the concept, explain the mechanism, describe the real-world impact. Three marks, three moves.

C
CONCEPT

Name the specific concept, technology, or principle the question is asking about. Be precise — vague openings lose the first mark.

MARK ONE
E
EXPLAIN

Describe how or why it works. The mechanism, not the definition. This is where most students stop — which is why most students score two out of three.

MARK TWO
I
IMPACT

Connect it to a real consequence — for the business, the user, the system. Specific and contextual. This is the mark that separates a good answer from a complete one.
